Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our digital health platform.

How do I access my medical records?

Sign in to your patient portal and open the records tab to see visits, results, and prescriptions in one place.

Is my health data secure and private?

Yes. The layout emphasizes secure access points, protected account flows, and privacy-first patient management.

Can I book virtual consultations?

Yes. You can select a doctor, choose a time slot, and use the platform for virtual or in-person visits.
